WORD - MASTERS

    RULES

  1. Guess the right word within 6 chances only.
  2. The word contains 5 letters that will be entered in the boxes above
  3. After taking your guess, press the Enter key to submit the word.
    • If the word is not a recognizable word from English dictionary (if you just type gibberish) the boxes flash red and you can take another guess for that chance.
    • If any letter exists in the right word and is in the right position , the corresponding box turns green.
    • If any letter exists in the right word but is in the wrong position , the corresponding box turns yellow.
    • If any letter doesn't exist in the right word , the box turns grey.
  4. Use the above clues and find out the right word, if you exhaust all your guesses, then you lose and the secret word is revealed to you.
  5. The secret word changes every day so come back the next day for another try :)

    6. HAPPY GUESSING!!
